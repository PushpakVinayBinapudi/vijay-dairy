About Vijay Dairy

Born from a Belief in Pure Milk

We started Vijay Dairy in Andhra Pradesh with one simple mission — to bring genuinely fresh, chemical-free dairy products directly to your home. No middlemen. No processing. No compromise.

Direct from Our Farm
