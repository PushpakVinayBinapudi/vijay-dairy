Our Story

Born from a Passion
for Pure Dairy

Vijay Dairy was founded with one belief — every family in Kakinada deserves honest, pure dairy. We started small with a few cows and a big dream. Today we serve hundreds of families, and our commitment to purity has never wavered.

🚜
Same Morning Delivery
Milk is collected and dispatched the same morning — no storage, no cold chain, no compromise on freshness.
🧪
Purity Guaranteed
We never mix water, powder, or chemicals. What leaves our farm is exactly what your family drinks.
🌿
Ethical & Sustainable
Our cows graze freely, eat natural fodder, and live healthy lives. Better animals mean better milk.
